The Challenge

Scaling outreach and onboarding across regulated verticals created operational inconsistency and heavy dependency on manual coaching.

Reps lacked clear prioritization, guidance was scattered, and leadership had limited visibility into execution.

The goal was to build a repeatable, scalable GTM system that drove consistency, speed, and quality.

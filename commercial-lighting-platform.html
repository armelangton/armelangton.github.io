Commercial Lighting Intelligence Platform

Challenge

A small commercial lighting distributor may need to collect requirements, assemble a bill of materials, request supplier pricing, compare quotes, protect margin, prepare proposals, and coordinate CRM updates.

Approach

I structured the workspace around quote readiness, supplier comparison, margin guidance, product information, and proposal preparation, with AI support for analysis and communication.

Solution

  • RFQ and bill of materials workspace
  • Supplier comparison and margin visibility
  • Product recommendation support
  • Proposal preparation and CRM coordination

The prototype shows how a connected workspace can make complex quoting information easier to evaluate and act on.

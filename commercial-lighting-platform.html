Portfolio project

Commercial Lighting Intelligence Platform

Business Application • AI-Assisted Quoting

Portfolio concept and prototype

Overview

A business application designed around the end-to-end quoting workflow of a small commercial lighting distributor.

The problem

One salesperson may need to collect project requirements, assemble a bill of materials, request supplier pricing, compare quotes, protect margin, prepare customer proposals, and coordinate CRM updates.

The approach

The platform centralizes quote readiness, supplier comparison, margin guidance, product information, and proposal preparation while using AI to assist with analysis and communication.

Platform ecosystem

  • RFQ and quote-readiness review
  • Bill of materials workspace
  • Supplier comparison
  • Margin guidance
  • Product recommendation support
  • Proposal preparation
  • CRM synchronization
  • AI-assisted supplier communication

What the project demonstrates

  • Business analysis
  • Product discovery
  • Workflow design
  • Internal application design
  • Requirements gathering
  • AI workflow design
  • Data and system thinking

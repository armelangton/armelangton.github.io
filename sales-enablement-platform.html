Sales Enablement Platform

Sales Enablement • Business Applications • AI Experiences

Overview

A business application that brings sales activity, account context, learning, knowledge, playbooks, coaching, and AI support into one operational environment.

What it does

  • Organizes daily priorities and next actions
  • Connects account and opportunity context
  • Supports calls, tasks, events, and notes
  • Embeds learning, playbooks, and knowledge
  • Adds AI guidance where work happens
  • Supports coaching, practice, and execution visibility

My role

I defined the business problem, workflow architecture, interface structure, content model, and AI use cases. I designed and iterated on the experience and used modern web technologies and AI-assisted development to build the working prototype.

Portfolio project

Sales Enablement Platform

Business Application • AI • Workflow Design

Portfolio concept and prototype

Overview

A business application designed to connect account activity, customer context, learning, knowledge, coaching, playbooks, and AI assistance in one operational experience.

The problem

Sales teams often work across CRM records, shared drives, training tools, playbooks, scripts, and AI assistants. Important guidance becomes difficult to find and disconnected from the moment it is needed.

The approach

The platform uses familiar CRM-style architecture as the operational backbone while embedding enablement directly into account, opportunity, activity, learning, and knowledge workflows.

Platform ecosystem

  • Account and opportunity workspaces
  • Activity management
  • Learning and knowledge
  • Playbooks and workflow guidance
  • AI objection handling
  • Discovery assistance
  • Meeting preparation
  • Research and communication support

What the project demonstrates

  • Product thinking
  • Workflow architecture
  • Information architecture
  • CRM experience design
  • AI integration
  • Learning and enablement design
  • UX iteration

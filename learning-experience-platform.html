Overview

A structured learning application designed for employee onboarding, knowledge reinforcement, practice, and measurable development.

The problem

Traditional corporate training can be passive, disconnected from actual work, and difficult to reinforce over time.

The approach

The platform combines structured learning paths with active practice and progress visibility.

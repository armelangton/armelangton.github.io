Fire Protection Platform

Business Applications • Field Workflow • AI Experiences

Overview

A field operations application that supports employees before, during, and after customer engagements through shared context, workflow guidance, documentation, resources, and embedded AI support.

What it does

  • Organizes pre-engagement context
  • Guides systems and checks
  • Supports current field activity
  • Captures readings, notes, photos, and issues
  • Provides AI field guidance
  • Connects post-engagement documentation and routing

My role

I defined the workflow, application structure, information hierarchy, interface direction, and AI use cases. I designed and iterated on the experience and used React, TypeScript, GitHub, and AI-assisted development to build the working prototype.

Portfolio project

Fire Protection Platform

AI Field Operations Platform

Portfolio concept and prototype

Overview

A field operations platform designed around the workflow of commercial fire protection employees before, during, and after customer engagements.

The problem

Customer history, site context, field tasks, resources, documentation, and post-engagement actions can be scattered across systems and conversations.

The approach

The platform creates one engagement workspace with pre-engagement context, a field workbench, post-engagement workflow, and embedded AI assistance.

Platform ecosystem

  • Pre-engagement briefing
  • Field workbench
  • Post-engagement workflow
  • Product knowledge
  • Training and continuing education
  • Recall research
  • Talking-point preparation
  • Follow-up assistance

What the project demonstrates

  • User and workflow research
  • Product design
  • Mobile workflow design
  • AI experience design
  • Information architecture
  • Front-end prototyping
  • Iterative design

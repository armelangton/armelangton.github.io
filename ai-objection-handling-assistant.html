AI Objection Handling Assistant

Challenge

Sales representatives often rely on memory, scattered documentation, or generic scripts during difficult conversations.

Approach

I organized the assistant around objection categories, persona and industry context, discovery guidance, response frameworks, and practical next actions instead of generic chatbot answers.

Solution

  • Structured objection scenarios
  • Contextual response guidance
  • Discovery questions and response frameworks
  • Recommended next actions and coaching

Demonstrated value

The assistant demonstrates how conversational AI can support preparation, practice, and more thoughtful responses in a focused sales workflow.

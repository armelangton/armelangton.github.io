Portfolio project

AI Objection Handling Assistant

AI Conversation Coaching Tool

Portfolio concept and prototype

Overview

A standalone AI assistant designed to help sales representatives prepare for and respond to common objections.

The problem

Representatives often rely on memory, scattered documentation, or generic scripts during difficult conversations.

The approach

The assistant uses structured objection categories, context, discovery guidance, and response frameworks to provide useful support rather than generic chatbot answers.

Product ecosystem

  • Objection categorization
  • Contextual responses
  • Discovery guidance
  • Persona and industry context
  • Supporting knowledge
  • Recommended next actions

What the project demonstrates

  • AI assistant design
  • Prompt and instruction architecture
  • Knowledge organization
  • Contextual response design
  • Sales workflow understanding
  • Practical AI adoption

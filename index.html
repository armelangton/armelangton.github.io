Case Study

Designing a Scalable Account Prioritization & Enablement Framework

With more than 13,000 contacts in HubSpot, scoring, filtering, and segmentation helped, but they did not fully solve the challenge of determining which accounts should receive the most attention, how frequently they should be worked, and which messaging approach made the most sense.

The Challenge

Sales reps needed a clearer way to prioritize effort across a large database while also improving confidence in more technical, compliance-driven conversations. Training alone was not enough — reps also needed reusable messaging, clearer value propositions, and more structured decision-making support.

The Approach

I helped develop an evolving prioritization and enablement framework that connected tiering logic, compliance/vertical tagging, messaging organization, and outreach workflows. The framework was refined iteratively over time as patterns became clearer and the team improved how accounts should be grouped, prioritized, and supported operationally.

Impact

  • Improved prioritization across a large contact database.
  • Reduced manual effort through reusable snippets, email templates, LinkedIn messaging, and call scripts.
  • Simplified complex compliance-related messaging for sales reps.
  • Increased consistency and confidence across outreach efforts.
  • Reduced time spent on onboarding, training, and repeated coaching.
  • Supported a more scalable approach to outreach, ABM targeting, and enablement execution.

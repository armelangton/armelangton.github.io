Case Study

Designing a Scalable Account Prioritization & Enablement Framework

Role: Lead contributor  |  Stack: HubSpot CRM, ZoomInfo, LinkedIn Sales Navigator

The Challenge

With more than 25,000 contacts in HubSpot, scoring and filtering helped — but didn't fully solve which accounts deserved the most attention, how frequently they should be worked, or which messaging approach made the most sense. Reps also needed support for more technical, compliance-driven conversations that training alone couldn't address.

The Approach

I helped develop an evolving prioritization and enablement framework that connected tiering logic, compliance and vertical tagging, messaging organization, and outreach workflows. The framework was refined iteratively over time as patterns emerged and the team improved how accounts should be grouped, prioritized, and supported operationally.

Tier 1

Focus: High-fit, compliance-driven accounts (~400 accounts)

Strategy: Higher-touch outreach, stronger personalization, and ABM alignment

Tier 2

Focus: Strong operational fit with identifiable business need (~2,500 accounts)

Strategy: Structured outreach supported by reusable messaging paths

Tier 3

Focus: Lower-priority or lower-urgency accounts (~4,200 accounts)

Strategy: Lower-frequency nurture and general awareness messaging

Impact

  • Helped segment and prioritize a database of ~25,000 contacts, identifying approximately 7,100 ICP-fit accounts across three tiers for structured outreach execution.
  • Developed 100+ email sequences and hundreds of outreach templates adopted across the full sales team.
  • Created ~20 vertical- and compliance-specific value propositions to help reps navigate more technical, high-stakes conversations with confidence.
  • Designed and delivered onboarding training and live demos that accelerated new rep ramp time by 30+ days.
  • Built a framework that remained operational and continued supporting new reps through team changes — reducing repeated coaching and onboarding effort.

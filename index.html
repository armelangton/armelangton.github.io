    Case Study

    Designing a Scalable Account Prioritization & Enablement Framework

    With more than 13,000 contacts in HubSpot, scoring, filtering, and segmentation helped, but they did not fully solve the day-to-day challenge of deciding which accounts should receive the most attention, how often they should be contacted, and which messaging path made the most sense.

    The Challenge

    Sales reps needed a clearer way to prioritize effort across a large database while also improving confidence in more complex, compliance-driven conversations. Technical training helped, but reps still needed practical value propositions, reusable messaging, and clearer decision logic.

    The Approach

    I helped develop an account tiering and enablement framework that connected prioritization logic, compliance/vertical tagging, messaging paths, and outreach strategy. The framework evolved over time as patterns became clearer and the team refined how accounts should be grouped, approached, and supported.

    Tier 1

    Focus: High-fit, compliance-driven accounts

    Strategy: Higher-touch outreach, stronger personalization, ABM alignment

    Tier 2

    Focus: Strong operational fit with clear business need

    Strategy: Structured outreach with reusable messaging paths

    Tier 3

    Focus: Lower-priority or lower-urgency accounts

    Strategy: Lower-frequency nurture and general awareness messaging

    Impact

    • Improved prioritization across a large contact database.
    • Reduced manual effort through reusable snippets, email templates, LinkedIn messages, and call scripts.
    • Made complex compliance messaging easier for reps to understand and apply.
    • Increased consistency across outreach and reduced time spent training others.
    • Supported a more scalable approach to sales execution, ABM targeting, and enablement.

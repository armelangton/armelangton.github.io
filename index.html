Case Study

Designing a Scalable Account Prioritization & Enablement Framework

Role: Lead contributor  |  Stack: HubSpot CRM · ZoomInfo · LinkedIn Sales Navigator

Built a scalable sales enablement and CRM prioritization framework that connected segmentation logic, compliance tagging, reusable messaging systems, onboarding resources, and outreach workflows across a 25,000+ contact database.

The Problem

As the sales organization scaled, the CRM had grown to more than 25,000 contacts across multiple industries, compliance categories, and levels of opportunity quality. While HubSpot scoring, filtering, and segmentation existed, reps still struggled with prioritization, outreach consistency, and navigating more technical compliance-related conversations.

The larger challenge was not just lead volume. It was creating a scalable operational system that connected prioritization, messaging, enablement, and execution in a way that reps could realistically use day-to-day.

The System

I helped develop an evolving framework that combined account tiering, compliance and vertical tagging, messaging paths, call scripts, snippets, LinkedIn messaging, email sequences, and onboarding resources.

This turned fragmented knowledge into a repeatable structure reps could use to make faster decisions, personalize outreach, and build confidence in more complex sales conversations.

Impact

  • Segmented and prioritized a 25,000+ contact database into clearer account tiers and outreach paths.
  • Created reusable messaging infrastructure across email, LinkedIn, call scripts, snippets, and value propositions.
  • Helped reps navigate technical compliance conversations with more confidence and consistency.
  • Reduced manual decision-making by connecting CRM structure, segmentation, and outreach logic.
  • Reduced repeated coaching and training needs by turning complex knowledge into scalable enablement resources.
  • Supported more consistent execution across sales outreach, onboarding, campaign planning, and ABM targeting.

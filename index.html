Process

A clear path from an unclear problem to a useful solution.

  1. DiscoverBegin with conversations to understand the business, the people, the needs, and the problem.
  2. StrategizeAnalyze what was learned, connect the relevant information, and define the right approach.
  3. DesignTranslate the strategy into workflows, messaging, interfaces, content, or solution requirements.
  4. BuildCreate the application, AI solution, CRM experience, automation, workflow, or enablement resource.
  5. DeployTest, prepare for real-world use, launch, gather feedback, and support future iteration when appropriate.

I begin by understanding people and their work. I combine analytical thinking, communication, creativity, and technology to give complex or abstract information useful structure and build practical solutions with a clear business purpose.

Understanding creates better solutions. Better solutions create better experiences. And there's always another opportunity to improve.

Frequently asked questions

Clear answers about scope, pricing, ownership, and what happens after launch.

Are all projects fixed fee?

Yes. The outcome, scope, deliverables, schedule, and price are agreed before work begins. If a new request falls outside that scope, it is presented as a separate fixed-fee addition.

Where should we start?

If the problem or requirements are not yet clear, the Sales Operations Assessment establishes the current state, priorities, and recommended path. A well-defined project may be scoped directly.

Do you work with the software we already use?

Yes. A solution may improve or connect existing systems rather than replace them. The approved scope identifies the platforms, data sources, and integrations involved.

Can the solution be expanded later?

Yes. Applications, assistants, workflows, and supporting documentation are designed so that approved capabilities can be added without unnecessarily rebuilding the original foundation.

Who owns the completed work?

You receive the agreed deliverables, documentation, and training included in the project scope. Third-party software, hosting, AI usage, and licenses remain subject to their providers' terms and charges.

Is ongoing support required?

No. Projects include a defined handoff. If ongoing maintenance, updates, or additional improvements are needed, they can be scoped separately at a fixed price.
